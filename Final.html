Assignment 8: Final Project

Project Pitch: Wedding Stuff

I'm getting married soon, like in 2 weeks...

For this assignment, I am taking a large moment in my life – my wedding, and using digital fabrication to make some useful stuff. STAKES ARE HIGH - kind of.

There are 2 parts of the project:

  1. Wedding Contract Framing Mat
  2. Cake Topper

Part 1: Wedding Contract Framing Mat

What is it? A framing mat for a ketubah, an official Jewish marriage contact.

I will use Rhino and Grasshopper, maybe Parrot... to design a parametric patterned frame mat, and laser cut the decorations. I plan on using 2 layers of paper on top of the document.


Part 2: Wedding Cake Topper

What is it? A little topper for the cake that looks like us.

I will use Rhino to add 3 meshes together. One existing one, and two facial scan of myself and my fiance. I will make a mold and 3D print it, cast the 3D print in OOMOO and cast small plaster version of us. I will then paint the little figurines to match our outfits.
